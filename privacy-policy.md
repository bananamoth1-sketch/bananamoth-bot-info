Privacy Policy — Bananamoth Bot
Last updated: June 20, 2025

What We Collect
When you interact with Bananamoth Bot, we may collect the following information:

User IDs — your Discord user ID
Usernames and display names — as they appear on Discord
Messages — Used for the ai chat system memory.. If you dont use this system no messages are stored (This only opperates in one channel in the bananamoth discord server, no other messages anywhere else are tracked)
Server and channel IDs — to identify where interactions occur
Role information — roles assigned or tracked during verification
Giveaway entries — When entering a giveaway we track your user id in a database to make sure your entered into the giveaway
Timestamps — when interactions and events occur such as the ai messaging feature

How We Use Your Data
We only use collected data to operate the bot's core features, including:

Tracking who has completed verification
Assigning the correct roles after verification
Running giveaways and selecting winners fairly
Disabling specific features in the ai system
Keeping your ai system message memory
Specific features that measure time before disabling things like interactive buttons
Tracking who picks what roles so users can remove the roles once decided they no longer want them


We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any purpose beyond running the bot. Most of your data can be removed by request.

How We Store Your Data:
Data is stored in files on the bot's private server. It is not shared with third parties, sold, or disclosed to anyone outside the development team. Access is limited to us bot developers only.

Data Retention
We retain data only as long as it is needed to operate the bot's features. If you request deletion, we will remove your data as described below.

Your Rights — Deleting Your Data
You have the right to request deletion of your data at any time. To do so, join our support server and contact a bot developer — we'll get your data cleared out as quickly as we can.
Support server: https://discord.gg/WAHVmn2jVQ -- Please note we wont just clear specific things, if you request for data to be cleared we will clear all of your data. 

Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions?
Join the support server and head to the #get-support channel — we're happy to help!
