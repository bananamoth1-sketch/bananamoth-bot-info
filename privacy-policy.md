# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 13, 2025**

Hey! here is what our bot bananamoth bot does with user data (data we collect listed below)


this is all we collect:
- Your **Discord User ID** so we know who's verified and who's entered giveaways
- Your **Server ID** so the bot knows where it's working
- A list of **giveaway entries** (just User IDs, nothing else)

That's it. No emails, no messages, no personal info.


What we do with it:
We only use it to run the bot's features:
- Keeping track of who's verified
- Running giveaways and picking winners
- Giving you the right role after verification

 Where it's stored:
Everything is saved in a simple file on the bot's server. It's not shared with anyone, not sold, not used for anything. Nobody else sees it.


Deleting your data:
Want your data gone? Just join the support discord server and try to get in contact with one of the bot developers and we will get to work on clearing out your data as fast as we can
